About

I unexpectedly fell in love with the power of design while working with less-than-stellar electronic health records in the ER at 3 am. While my career goals have changed, my drive to improve the human experience has not. I design because I believe that progress, even in seemingly impossible challenges, is possible with the right design process. I am a dynamic problem solver who can successfully pinpoint the most desirable and feasible solution. I firmly believe learning from those the design will impact is crucial to creating successful experiences.
