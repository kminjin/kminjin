Why do I design?

I design because like myself, design is inherently optimistic. I believe progress, even in large and intractable challenges, is possible with the right design process. I am a dynamic problem solver who is able to open up to vast creative possibilities but then can successfully zero in on what is the most desirable, feasible, and viable for the people + company I am designing for. If done thoughtfully, design has the power to improve people's lives in both big and small ways.
