73 words about me & my design process

I design because like myself, design is inherently optimistic. I believe progress, even in seemingly-impossible challenges, is possible with the right design process. I am a dynamic problem solver who can open up to vast creative possibilities but then successfully zeroes in on what is the most desirable and viable for the people + company I am designing for. To create thoughtful experiences that can improve human experiences, I believe we need to learn from the humans the design will impact.
